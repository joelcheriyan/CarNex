Safety

Safety for Passengers

All of our drivers have at least a G licence and have undergone intensive criminal check by CSIS to ensure that they have not committed any criminal offence that could pose harm to themselves or their passengers. Users of CarNex are required to verify their phone number at signup.

In the extremely unlikely event of physical or sexual assault, CarNex will pay for all expenses necessary for the victim to recover. CarNex will also cooperate with the authorities by providing all personal details of the driver upon request.

It is always advisable to let a family member or friend know that you are going on a trip and by what means so they can contact the police and CarNex if they donâ€™t hear from you.

Safety for Drivers

Every driver has the option to purchase our insurance at the beginning of any contract which may cover up to 80% of the market price if any harm is done to their car by passengers.

Every registered passenger has verified their phone number with us, allowing them to be found if something goes wrong. We will ensure that every driver gets paid the agreed price for their service.
