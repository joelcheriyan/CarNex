How it Works

Don't Worry, You're Safe With Us.

CarNex is deeply invested in the safety of each and every one of our users. You control what's shown on your profile to other users of the service, and to people who haven't made an account. By using our integrated chat service to communicate with people you might want to share a ride with, you can keep your private information to yourself until you're ready to share. All they'll be able to see is what you make available to them.

No Postings? No Problem!

Don't worry if nobody's posted looking to share a ride for a trip that meets your criteria yet. It's easy to put up a request of your own. Just pick a public place near the location you want to depart from, and another public place that's near your destination. CarNex will make it easy for people who want to make the same trip to find you.

Easy as One, Two, Three.

Here at CarNex, we want to make organizing your commute as easy as can be. Just make an account, search through postings near your planned route, and contact posters through our integrated chat system once you're ready. It's that simple!
