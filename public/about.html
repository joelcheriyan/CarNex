Our Mission

Our story

The team behind CarNex has a dream of improving the lives of commuters and car owners in Canada. It was launched on May 31, 2014 by five young entrepreneurs who wished to contribute to an environmental cause by providing an efficient solution to facilitate carpooling between citizens. CarNex is strongly devoted to promoting carpooling as an alternate mode of transportation to driving alone, taking a taxi, or using public transit systems.

Overview

Our website is primarily built to connect people wishing to commute to a common destination. It acts as an intermediary between registered members and does not provide transportation for people. CarNex has many objectives such as reducing money and time spent by individuals driving alone, reducing the number of vehicles on the roads, helping ease traffic congestion and lowering emissions of greenhouse gases. In addition, we aim to collaborate with some transit agencies to offer a complementary mode of transportation to their existing network.
