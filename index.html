CarNex

People Moving People.

What We Do

CarNex is devoted to promoting carpooling as an alternate mode of transportation to driving alone, taking a taxi, or using public transit systems. Our website is primarily built to connect people wishing to commute to a common destination.

Safety

At CarNex, we are dedicated to ensuring that all the trips organised through the site are as safe as can be. We want to ensure that both our drivers and passengers can be vouched for to make sure everyone involved is comfortable.

Meet the Team

We are a team of students from the University of Toronto. We are very excited about the future of web programming and how we can use this technology to provide services to people.

The Perfect Pair. Drivers, Meet Passengers.

CarNex brings people looking to share a car ride together. Whether you're hoping to share the price of gas on a long road trip or just hoping for a new friend on your daily commute, CarNex can help.

Here, There, Everywhere.

CarNex allows you to find people traveling from anywhere, to anywhere. There are no set destinations. Wherever you want to go, CarNex can help you find someone else who is going there too.

Pay What You Like. Like What You Pay.

With CarNex, there are no minimum prices or extra fees. Fares are negotiated strictly between drivers and passengers. Say hello to the easiest and most affordable way to travel.
