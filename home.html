How it works

CarNex is devoted to promoting carpooling as an alternate mode of transportation to driving alone, taking a taxi, or using public transit systems. Our website is primarily built to connect people wishing to commute to a common destination.

Safety

At CarNex we want to ensure that all the trips organised throught the site are as safe as can be. We want to ensure that both our drivers and passengers can be vouched for to make sure everyone involved is comfortable.

The Perfect Pair, Drivers meet Passengers.

CarNex helps to bring people looking to share a car ride together. Whether you're hoping to share the price of gas on a long roadtrip or just hoping for a new friend on your daily commute, CarNex will help match drivers to passengers.

Here, There, Everywhere.

CarNex allows you to find people travelling to any location. There are no set destinations. Wherever you want to go, CarNex will help you find someone else who is going there too.

Pay what you like. Like what you pay.

With CarNex there are no minimum prices or extra fees. Fares are negotiated strictly between drivers and passengers. The easiest and most affordable way to travel.
